What is SINUHE?

HieroJIS and Marwan’s font were combined and named SINUHE (Sublime INput of Unicode for Hieroglyphic Egyptian). And you can type hieroglyphs like this 𓅭𓈖𓉔𓏏𓆮𓀀 faster ane easier.

Who is making SINUHE?

Marwan Kilani (font), So Miyagawa (input system, webpage, advertisement), Camilla Di Biase-Dyson (coaching), Fabian Wespi (Demotic version), David Chapman (general), Steven Feuer (general) and you can freely join our team!

Faster and Easier. Four ways of inputting.

  1. transliteration of a word including phonetic complemetns and the determinative: snb = 𓋴𓈖𓃀𓆃𓏛
  2. category like MAN, GOD, MAMMAL...: MAMMAL = 𓃢,𓃥,𓃒,𓃼,𓃮,𓃶,𓃟...
  3. transliteration of phonograms:　nfr + f + r = 𓄤𓆑𓂋
  4. Gardiner’s code: A1 + B1 + Z2 ＝　𓀀𓁐𓏥

Implementation of Quadrat Writing

You can change colours of texts, underline glyphs and search and replace them.

Works on Office, Notepad, everywhere.

Works very well on Notepad, Libre Office, Open Office, iWorks (Pages, Numbers, Keynote), Office 2011 for Mac, Office 2013, Sublime Text 3, Cot Editor, Notes (Mac), Goole Chrome (requires settings), Firefox
